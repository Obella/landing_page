Your Baby's Health Stats-Now Accessable by You

Your pregnancy is an exciting time, but also your first encounter with worry as a mom. To make this unique journey as comfortable and joyful as possible, you need the best information to help it go smoothly and safely.
Today, moms like you have lots of information and resources at their disposal, but the information is often disjointed and objective.

If you had a solution that could collect the significant information particular to your own body and all of the wonderful changes you are experiencing, would you use it? What about a system that allows you to record the thrilling experience of your baby’s kicks and movements and reminds you of important appointments and checks you need to make? Introducing Obella Be – Comfort. Knowledge. Confidence for your pregnancy. Easy to wear and always with you, Obella Be integrates sensor data and a record of all the events you consider important about your baby’s development and health.

Obella Be provides moms:
Reminders for appointments, scheduled medications, measurements to take such as blood pressure to keep you aware of the crucial changes you need to know at any time. A world-wide community of moms to answer your most pressing questions, share experiences and give access to a library of information at your fingertips. Are you ready to take charge of your pregnancy and make it the best life’s journey that it is? Then join the Obella Be network of moms today.
